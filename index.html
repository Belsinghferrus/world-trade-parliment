About Us

A New Era for Global Trade: The World Trade Parliament


We are the World Trade Parliament (WTP), a unique, non-governmental, business-centric LLC, leading the charge for ethical and equitable global commerce.

Our Purpose

To foster a global economic ecosystem where true capitalism – characterized by fair competition and widespread entrepreneurial opportunity – flourishes, thereby lifting individuals from poverty and empowering nations with genuine freedom. We are fundamentally opposed to crony capitalism, which enslaves governments, disrupts markets, produces poor-quality jobs, and ultimately stifles the universal human potential for business creation and innovation.

Our Vision

To be the preeminent global force for inclusive capitalism, envisioning a world teeming with a high quality and vast quantity of diverse business people, rather than a few powerful entities dominating markets. We strive for a future where every individual, regardless of nationality or background, has an equal chance to contribute to the global economy as an entrepreneur, leading to truly free governments and prosperous nations unburdened by undue influence.

Our Mission

Dismantling Crony Capitalism:

Actively combating structures that allow a select few to control governments, secure preferential funding, waive colossal debts, and burden entrepreneurs.

Protecting Traders in Conflict Zones

Challenging punitive trade taxes on innocent businesses affected by geopolitical conflicts.

Promoting Entrepreneurial Growth

Championing high-quality business opportunities to increase the global population of independent business people.

Ensuring Government Independence

Fostering markets where a multitude of capitalists prevents the enslavement of public institutions by powerful interests.

Equal Opportunity for All

Amplifying the voice of business opportunities for every nationality, ensuring merit, not political sway, dictates success.

Global Networking and Collaboration

Facilitating an inclusive ecosystem for shared growth and genuine economic freedom through WBIZNEX.

Our Structure


Empowering the World's Business Voices: Our Two-House Parliament

The WTP employs an innovative two-house parliamentary system, designed for comprehensive representation, direct grassroots input, and effective policy formulation that directly engages governments.

House 1

The Global Business Council (GBC)

The Voice of Global Entrepreneurs

This house, composed of WBIZNEX members from around the world, is the vibrant forum where real-world business challenges are identified, issues are debated, and preliminary resolutions are forged from the ground up. It ensures the authentic voice of diverse entrepreneurs shapes our agenda.

  • Global representation
  • Issue identification
  • Solution brainstorming
  • Resolution drafting

House 2

The Global Policy & Diplomatic Council (GPDC)

Shaping Policy, Engaging Diplomacy

Composed primarily of WIIBA-trained members, this house meticulously refines GBC resolutions into actionable policy proposals. It serves as WTP's diplomatic arm, engaging directly with government representatives and diplomats to present our proposals and advocate for their adoption, ensuring our voice is heard where decisions are made.

  • Policy structuring
  • Diplomatic negotiation
  • Leveraging WIIBA expertise
  • Engagement with government views

Our Unique Voting System

Our voting on issues is restricted to members whose businesses or regions are directly and demonstrably affected by the matter. This prevents external "muscle power" and ensures fair, relevant outcomes, prioritizing genuine impact over geopolitical influence.

Our Wing


Empowering Your Global Journey

The Pillars of World Trade Parliament
Action Through WBIZNEX & WIIBA

WBIZNEX (World Business Nexus)

Connect, Collaborate, Conquer:
The Global Business Network

WBIZNEX is WTP's dynamic business networking wing, fostering unparalleled global connections. It provides the platform for business people worldwide to connect, share insights, find opportunities, and become active members of the Global Business Council.

Extensive B2B networking, access to global markets, collective problem-solving, pathway to GBC membership.

WIIBA (World Institute of International Business Accomplishments)

Learn, Explore, Lead
Mastering International Business

WIIBA is WTP's transformative educational arm, dedicated to cultivating high-quality, future-proof international business leaders. Our unique 24-month program combines in-depth country-specific market learning with immersive practical exposure across 24 nations, covering international trade laws, rules, and economics.

Unrivaled global market mastery, practical business connections, leadership development, pathway to GPDC membership, preparing entrepreneurs to thrive in a fair global economy.

Our Core Principles


Standing for True Capitalism & Global Integrity

The World Trade Parliament Pledge: Fairness, Freedom, and Entrepreneurial Power

Against Cronyism & Enslaved Governments

We stand against systemic corruption where powerful individuals manipulate governments, obtain massive debt waivers, and impose unfair tax burdens, thereby stifling genuine entrepreneurship and undermining national constitutions. We advocate for governments to be free from undue influence, serving the people, not a privileged few.

Protecting Business from Geopolitical Fallout

We actively work to shield innocent traders and businesses from the devastating financial impacts of conflicts they are not responsible for, opposing punitive trade taxes and protecting their livelihoods.

For a Multitude of Business People

We believe true economic strength comes from a thriving population of independent, high-quality entrepreneurs, not a system that prioritizes a few large entities. Our mission is to increase the number of diverse business people creating wealth and opportunity worldwide.

Upholding Constitutional Dignity

We advocate for the supremacy of national constitutions and the rule of law against leaders who seek to elevate personal power through illicit means, ensuring dignity and freedom for every nation and its population.

Promoting Openness & Mutual Growth

We champion transparency in trade, equitable access to opportunities, and collaborative practices that enrich knowledge and foster mutual growth for all.
